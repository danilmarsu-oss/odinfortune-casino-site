Recognising Problem Gambling

We want to help our players recognise the early signs of problem gambling. If any of the following statements apply to you, we strongly encourage you to reach out for help:

  • You spend more money on gambling than you can afford to lose.
  • You find it difficult to stop gambling once you have started.
  • You gamble to escape stress, anxiety, or personal problems.
  • You lie to friends or family about how much time or money you spend gambling.
  • You feel irritable or restless when you try to cut down on gambling.
  • You borrow money or sell possessions to fund your gambling.
  • Your gambling is negatively affecting your work, relationships, or mental health.

Recognising these signs is the first and most important step towards getting help. We are here to support you every step of the way.
